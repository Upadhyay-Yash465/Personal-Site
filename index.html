Dance · KnightRaas

Nationals, 2026.

UCF KnightRaas · Competitive Garba-Raas · Captain Intern

Garba and Raas are classical folk forms from Gujarat — circle dances rooted in devotion, now refined into a competitive circuit judged on synchronization, footwork, formation, and cultural authenticity. The season runs through winter. Nationals is the final tournament, and we won it.

As Captain Intern I sit at the junction between the floor and leadership: choreography input, formation calls, member accountability, comp-day floor management. Garba-Raas is also the most culturally specific thing I do — South Asian diaspora expression in a competitive frame, with real weight behind it.

Mock Trial

Competitive Legal Advocacy.

UCF Mock Trial · Attorney & Witness

Mock trial is courtroom simulation at a competitive level — opening statements, witness examination, objections, closing arguments, judged by real attorneys. It demands the same skill set as debate but in a different frame: legal reasoning, rapid adaptation, and the ability to control a room under pressure.

I came into it through speech and debate, which gave me the argument structure. Mock trial added the adversarial dimension — you don't just make your case, you dismantle someone else's in real time.
